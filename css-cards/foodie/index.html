Kolkata Biriyani

Kolkata biryani is a royal, fragrant, and milder variant of Awadhi cuisine, famously defined by its inclusion of potatoes and boiled eggs along with aromatic rice and tender meat.

Kathi Roll

Kolkata kathi rolls are an iconic street food originating in the 1930s at Nizam's. Popular variations include egg, chicken, mutton, and paneer rolls.

Payesh

Payesh is a quintessential Bengali, made with milk, sugar/jaggery, and Gobindobhog rice, symbolizing love and blessings. It is a mandatory dish for birthdays, Annaprashan (first rice), and festivals like Durga Puja.

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Khichdi

Bengali khichuri is a nostalgic dish, fried hilsa fish or roasted khichuri with hot beeguni is incomparable on a rainy day. It is not just a simple dish, but a comforting meal made with a mixture of Govind Bhog rice and moong dal

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

❤️

Sorshe Ilish

Shorshe Ilish (Hilsa in mustard gravy) is the ultimate Bengali monsoon emotion, blending the intense pungent aroma of mustard with delicate, oily fish.
